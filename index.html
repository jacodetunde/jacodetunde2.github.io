Insurance Marketing Engagement

In this project, I used descriptive analysis to draw insight into the customer behavior of an insurance company to their marketing calls. I used logistic regression to understand what attributes of customers drive higher engagement rates. I started by analyzing the data to better understand and identify noticeable patterns in the data using matplotlib and pandas to visualize the data and then run logistic regression. The results of the logistic regression show that, Income, Monthly Premium Auto, Months Since Last Claim, Months Since Policy Inception , Number of Open Complaints, Number of Policies, and Gender variables are significant at 0.05 and have a negative relationship with engagement. That means the higher the customer's income, the less likely the customer will be engaged with marketing calls.
